Testing Standards

Product Testing Standards

Rigorous testing protocols ensuring product performance and safety

Impact Resistance Testing

  • Ball drop test: 227g steel ball from 3.9m height
  • Penetration resistance per ANSI Z26.1
  • High-velocity projectile testing
  • Temperature cycling impact testing

Weatherability Testing

  • QUV accelerated weathering (1000+ hours)
  • UV resistance & yellowing resistance
  • Temperature range: -40°C to +85°C
  • Humidity & salt spray corrosion testing

Optical Performance Testing

  • Light transmission: 92% (clear)
  • Haze measurement: <1% maximum
  • Distortion & refractive quality check
  • Spectrophotometer color verification
