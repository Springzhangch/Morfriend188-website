About Morfriend Manufacturing

Established in 2000, a leading global manufacturer of premium golf cart windshields with 27+ years of engineering excellence

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2000

MORFRIEND EV TECH CO., LTD., operating under the brand Morfriend, is a professional manufacturer specializing in high-quality golf cart windshields. Located in Dongguan, Guangdong, China's manufacturing heartland, we have grown from a small workshop to a globally recognized industry leader.

With over two decades of experience, we have perfected our manufacturing processes and quality control systems. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of partners in more than 50 countries worldwide.

We specialize in acrylic and polycarbonate windshields for all major golf cart brands including Club Car, EZGO, and YAMAHA. Our state-of-the-art facility combines advanced technology with skilled craftsmanship to deliver products that exceed industry standards.
