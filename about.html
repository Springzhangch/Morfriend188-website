About Morfriend Manufacturing

Established in 2000, a leading global manufacturer of premium golf cart windshields with 27+ years of engineering excellence

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2000

MORFRIEND EV TECH CO., LTD. (operating under the brand Morfriend) is a leading professional manufacturer specializing in high-quality golf cart windshields. Headquartered in Dongguan, Guangdong—China’s premier manufacturing hub—we have evolved from a specialized workshop into a globally recognized industry supplier.

With over 20 years of manufacturing experience, we deliver durable clear and tinted windshield solutions designed for maximum visibility and impact resistance.

We manufacture premium acrylic and polycarbonate windshields for Club Car, EZGO, YAMAHA and other EV golf cart brands. We ship durable, factory-direct fold-down and one-piece designs to B2B partners in 50+ countries.
